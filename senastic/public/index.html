Hello!
My name is Diana Sena Avoke and i deal with combine technical and inventive modelling and painting techniques by transforming wire mesh into a multiplicity of projected shapes and cubic forms affixed onto a support or space.

My works engages with the idea of order and disorder, and the subjection of perfected shapes into an amorphous finish.
